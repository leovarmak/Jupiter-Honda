Two wheeler Technician Vocational Training Program

Honda 2wheelers set up “Honda Technical centre of Excellence” under Skill India vision to train poor & needy unemployed youth to generate livelihood. HMSI has set up technical vocational training centres at 5 locations i.e. Karnal, Pune, Bangalore, Lucknow, and Bhubaneswar. The plan is to skill up 3000 youth from these 5 centres with courses as 2wheeler Technician, Supervisor & Advisor.

This course implemented by our training partner Labournet services India Pvt. Ltd. And certified by NSDC, will skill up youth to work with corporates, Dealers, & Self Enterprises.

The process of technical vocational training centre is to cater sourcing (mobilization and counselling), registration, training delivery (centre based learning and on the job training) and certified by NSDC after completion of course.

Environment Project Harit Udaan

The world is going through drastic climatic changes, In that scenario Honda Motorcycle and Scooter India Pvt. Ltd (HMSI) with SankalpTaru foundation pledged to increase the green cover of denuded areas & successfully planted 100,000 trees various villages near all Factories in Gujarat, Karnataka, Rajasthan, Haryana, across 4 states of India. These are fruit bearing trees and was carried out at places where socio- environment impact could be maximized. These trees are being Geo Tagged and dash board being created in website for ensuring higher transparency, ease of monitoring & reporting. HMSI thru its implementation partner SankalpTaru Foundation is working closely with beneficiaries to create a self-enthusiastic and self-sustainable ecosystem.

Project ‘Harit Udaan’ targets to create a self-sustainable & empowered ecosystem wherein community members are engaged in growing and nurturing fruit bearing trees in the long run.

  1. Automated Drip Irrigation < More crop per drop >.

  4. Latest technical knowhow to farmers.
  5. Focus on Disabled & Women beneficiaries.
  6. Geo Tagging of plants, beneficiaries < Monitoring ease >
  7. Appreciation by IICA-Min of Corp Affairs & Karnataka State Mango Development & Marketing Corp.

Healthcare to Hut - To build a robust EMR Collection & telemedicine Initiative
Honda started a revolutionary step bringing healthcare to the villages with an innovative testing device named Swasthyslate, this health activity concentrate on the creation of a patient’s history and chemistry for treatment to be made possible and then connect to Specialists living far away from the patients. So the project uses Motorcycle borne paramedics armed with Tablets and Swasthya Slate (the low cost powerful device that allows Android Tablets & Phones to conduct 33 diagnostic tests like blood pressure, Blood group, Blood Hemoglobin, pulse oxymetry, BMI, thyroid , Malaria , Syphilis & ECG etc. on the mobile device). The results are then uploaded to the Cloud. Back-end data mining will segregate the information to UIDAI (Unique Identity Authority of India) and those not registered will have it in Cloud based vaults. The former now gets the information to store against the person’s records. (In our last filed Patent the UIDAI collaborated).Those who need immediate attention are now put on a call to the Specialists in a city location on the Tablet. Wherever the mobile signal doesn’t allow for this, a recording is made of the patient complaints, shared later in signal positive areas and the findings and the diagnosis communicated physically by the Paramedic rider.
In first phase we started in 10 nos. of villages of Rajasthan from 11th December 2015 & till 31.01.2016, we covered approx. 6619 nos. of beneficiaries’ villagers & now we extended the same facility in 10 nos. of villages of Ahmedabad near vithalapur area from 23rd January 2016 & covers approx. 500 nos. till 31.01.2016
