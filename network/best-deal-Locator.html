Best Deal Locator

As a pioneer, Honda introduced the concept of Certified Pre-owned Outlets - Best Deal. This allows us to create more touch points of interacting with customers. We facilitate complete solution to customers under one roof by extending Buy, Sell & Exchange facility. Honda Best Deal gives an opportunity to customer to upgrade to a New Honda with complete peace of Mind as we take care of complete transfers. Vehicles of any make are exchanged.

At the same time, Honda provides a refurbished & certified used Two Wheeler to a pre-owned buyer. Honda’s Quality, Reliability & Trust provides, “Joy to the Customer” through refurbishment done by trained technicians with genuine spares. The confidence is further enhanced with 6 Months Warranty* & 2 Free Services*.
